Welcome to Birwa Centre

Nestled in the serene locale of Melkar-Panemangalore (Bantwal Taluk, Dakshina Kannada), Birwa Centre is your perfect destination for a stay that blends comfort, convenience and calm. With well-appointed rooms, both A/C and non-A/C, modern amenities, ample parking and a peaceful setting away from city chaos, we make you feel right at home.

Whether you're here for business meetings, family gatherings or a restful getaway, our dedicated team ensures warm hospitality, pure vegetarian and non-vegetarian dining options, a roof-garden, auditorium and versatile event spaces to suit your every need.
